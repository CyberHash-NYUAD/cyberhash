Quantum Computing Workshop


Edison Murairi (B.S. in Physics and Computer Science, NYUAD '20), an incoming PhD candidate for the Physics program at George Washington University will host a Quantum Computing workshop on Saturday at 11am exclusively for Cyber#. This a great opportunity for our SIG and we strongly encourage you to attend.

Quantum computers could spur the development of new breakthroughs in cyber security, machine learning methods to diagnose illnesses sooner, materials to make more efficient devices and structures, financial strategies to live well in retirement, and algorithms to quickly direct resources such as ambulances. But what exactly is quantum computing, and what does it take to achieve these quantum breakthroughs?

When: Saturday, May 9th, 11 a.m.
